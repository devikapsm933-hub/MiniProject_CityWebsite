About Paris

Paris is the capital of France, famous for its art, history, landmarks, and delicious food. Known as the "City of Light," it is a hub for culture and tourism.

Famous Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The most iconic landmark of Paris with stunning views.

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of art masterpieces.

Notre Dame

Historic cathedral with Gothic architecture and rich history.

Famous Food

Croissants

Flaky buttery pastries loved worldwide.

Cheese

France is famous for its variety of delicious cheeses.

Macarons

Colorful and sweet almond meringue cookies.
